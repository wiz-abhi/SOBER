# Incident Communications

How we keep humans informed during a SEV.

## Channels
- `#incidents` — the operational channel. Every action and decision goes here, timestamped.
- Status page — updated by the incident commander within 10 minutes of a customer-facing SEV.

## Roles
- **Incident Commander (IC):** owns the response, delegates, makes the call to roll back.
- **Comms lead:** owns the status page and customer messaging.
- **Scribe:** keeps the timeline for the postmortem.

The IC is explicitly **not** the person with hands on the keyboard — separate command from execution so nobody is both debugging and coordinating.
